CASA VIRTUDE, PORTO

About About

Located in the historic center of Porto, the Casa Virtude Restaurant is a great welcoming space where cod is reinvented and served in a simple but irreverent way. With migas and baked potatoes, confit, fried in tempura, the cod is delicious and will be the delight of all who want to peek at the beautiful Passeio das Virtudes.
Opening hours: Tuesday to Saturday from 12:00 to 15:00 and Monday to Saturday from 19:00 to 24:00

SOLAR DO BACALHAU

About About

Solar do Bacalhau is a restaurant in the center of Coimbra that celebrates the local cuisine and serves this fish in a sublime and unique way. With a wood-burning oven that enhances the flavor of the dishes prepared there, such as baked cod in an alheira crust or cod in bread, it is a spacious and bright space where you can enjoy a genuine meal in the company of friends or family. Cod à lagareiro, Transmontana style, pataniscas, fish stew and cod rice are other specialties at this restaurant that you will want to try.
Opening hours: every day from 12:00 to 15:00 and from 19:00 to 23:00

LAURENTINA, O REI DO BACALHAU

About About

Now in Lisbon, we invite you to visit the Laurentina Restaurant, The King of Codfish, where, since 1976, delicious dishes not only Portuguese but also of Mozambican origin have been served. In a cozy atmosphere, you will find cod at its best, made in different ways, such as à brás, à Laurentina and with cream and spinach. We suggest that you also try bacalhau couvada à Pereira da Laurentina, a traditional Portuguese dish and a specialty of the house, prepared in the way of Beira Interior with products from Cova da Beira.
Opening hours: every day from 12:00 to 23:00

RESTAURANT O FUSO

About About

Nestled in the center of the village of Arruda dos Vinhos, still the District of Lisbon, O Fuso is a restaurant where dishes celebrate Portuguese cuisine with authenticity. As a result of the restoration of an old winery, this is a space where the Portuguese soul becomes palpable and, without a doubt, one of the best restaurants for eating cod. Prepared with quality ingredients and roasted over the coals, the cod in the coals à Fuso, seasoned with olive oil and raw garlic, is the speciality of this space and promises to make you want to return to this rustic and cozy corner in an equally picturesque village.
Opening hours: every day from 12.00 to 23.00

VARANDA DO CASAL

About About

Located in Casal de São Simão, a beautiful Aldeia do Xisto with a single street that extends along a ridge almost parallel to the course of the Ribeira de Alge, the Varanda do Casal Restaurant is a modern space where various traditional dishes are served in an appropriate way. genuine and tasty. So, come sit down at a table in one of the best restaurants to eat cod in Aldeias do Xisto, with an amazing view to the village and the valley, and immerse yourself in a sensory adventure.
Opening hours: from July to September from Tuesday to Sunday and from October to June from Wednesday to Sunday
